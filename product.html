Removes baked on carbon, oil and grease from Griddles, Fryers, BBQ, Ovens and Grills

3D Eco Oven and Grill Cleaner

3D Eco Oven and Grill cleaner is a combination of environmentally safe, water soluble and biodegradable solvents, surfactants emulsifying and corrosion inhibiting agents which penetrate deep into the surface and remove baked on carbon, food residue, grease, oil, fats, sugars and proteins. 3D Eco Oven and Grill cleaner solubulises, emulsifies and lifts up organic soils without damaging the cooking and baking equipments in hotels, restaurants, commercial kitchens, bakeries and other food manufacturing facilities. Biodegradable, water soluble and non abrasive formulation is safe on stainless steel and most of the other non porous hard surfaces.

  • Application

    Ovens, grills, deep fryers, rangers, roasters, hoods and other cooking equipments.

  • Instructions

    Apply directly on the surface and allow the solution to penetrate and solubulises the organic soil for 3-5 minutes. Brush or scrub the surface and rinse the surface thoroughly with warm water. For heavy baked on grease and oil, warm the oven (110 F) for 10-15 minutes, agitate and then rinse as indicated.

  • Dilution

    1:2 for heavy carbon or food deposit

    1:5 for normal soil, oil or food deposits.

    1:10 General equipment, hoods, vents and hard surface cleaning

  • Precautions

    Keep out of reach of children. Avoid contact with skin and eyes. Always wear gloves, body and eye protection. Always read MSDS before use. Never mix chemicals together without proper instructions only use those chemicals which are properly labeled. Report spills and accidents immediately.

  • First Aid

    Eyes: Flush with plenty of water if comes in contact with eyes. Get medical attention if irritation persists. Skin: Wash exposed area with large amount of water. Remove contaminated clothing and wash before reuse. Contact physician if irritation persists. Ingestion: Give large amount of water. Do not induce vomiting. Contact physician for further instruction. Never give anything by mouth to an unconscious person. Inhalation: Remove individual to fresh air if individual feels difficulty in breathing and seek medical advice immediately. Keep out of the reach of the children.
